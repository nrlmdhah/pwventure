Who We Are

Perfect Worldwide Venture Sdn. Bhd. (1069525-H) is a Bumiputra company founded on 8 November 2013. We provide flexible lending services to people who may not have access to traditional banks. Our aim is to help both lenders and borrowers. Our team of experienced directors and professionals ensures we deliver high-quality services on time.

Mission

Our mission is to provide clear, flexible, and customer-focused lending services. We aim to offer timely loans that help our clients reach their goals, while promoting responsible lending for long-term financial well-being.

Vision

To empower individuals and businesses by providing accessible, reliable, and affordable financial solutions, fostering growth and stability in the communities we serve.
